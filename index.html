The volunteers of the organization is going door to door handing over the food so that the people who are in crisis do not need to come out of their homes. We managed to support +3000 families with whatever fund we managed.

Shelter for a Family

A large number of families facing housing crisis in flood affected area. Thousands of families with pregnent women and unfed children are living under an open sky. so we are moving those families in safty and trying our level best to provide shelters.
